Privacy Policy

Last updated 1 November 2025 · governed by the NZ Privacy Act 2020

OraInvoice ("we", "us") provides workshop and trades management software to New Zealand businesses. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our website, app, and customer-facing payment pages.

1. What we collect

We collect information you provide and information generated as you use the service:

  • Account data — name, email, phone, business details and login credentials.
  • Customer & job data — records your business stores in OraInvoice (customers, vehicles, invoices, jobs).
  • Payment data — processed by Stripe; we store only tokens and the last four digits of a card.
  • Usage data — device, browser, IP address and interactions, used to keep the service secure and reliable.

2. How we use it

We use personal information to provide and improve the service, process payments, send transactional messages (such as invoices and booking reminders), prevent fraud, and meet our legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Sharing

We share data only with sub-processors who help us run the service — including Stripe (payments), Postmark and Twilio (messaging), and our cloud hosting provider — all bound by confidentiality and data-protection terms. Where you connect Xero or a calendar, data is shared with that provider under your direction.

4. Storage & security

Data is hosted in Australia/New Zealand region data centres with encryption in transit and at rest, daily backups, and replicated standby databases. Access is restricted, logged, and protected by mandatory two-factor authentication for staff.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you may request access to, or correction of, your personal information. Business account holders control the customer data they store; we act as a processor for that data. You can export or request deletion of your organisation's data at any time, subject to a 30-day grace period.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and a small number of analytics cookies to understand product usage. We do not use advertising trackers. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

7. Contact

Questions or a privacy request? Email our Privacy Officer at privacy@orainvoice.co.nz or write to OraInvoice, 14 Hone Heke Rd, Kerikeri 0230, New Zealand. You may also contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner if you are not satisfied with our response.
